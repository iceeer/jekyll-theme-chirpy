Subversion

对SVN瘦身,去掉老版本:

svnadmin dump /path/to/current/repo -r9000:10000 > svn.dump
svnadmin create /path/to/new/repo
svnadmin load /path/to/new/repo svn.dump Version 9000 and below are gone. The repository is now at version 1000. 链接： http://www.blogjava.net/BearRui/archive/2009/10/22/hudson-maven-svn.html Hudson+Maven+SVN 快速搭建持续集成环境 http://gavin-chen.iteye.com/blog/336607 Maven plugin中的lifecycle、phase、goal、mojo概念及作用的理解 http://trinea.iteye.com/blog/1290898 maven用途、核心概念、用法、常用参数和命令、扩展
